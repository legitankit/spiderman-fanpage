About The Legend

Origin Story

Teenager Peter Parker, bitten by a radioactive spider, gains arachnid-like powers. After failing to stop a burglar who later kills his Uncle Ben, Peter learns that "with great power comes great responsibility" — the creed that defines Spider-Man.

Creators

Writer Stan Lee and artist Steve Ditko introduced Spider-Man in Amazing Fantasy #15 (1962). A revolutionary hero — young, relatable, and masked — changing comics forever.

Comic History

Decades of iconic arcs: "The Night Gwen Stacy Died", "Kraven's Last Hunt", "Spider-Verse". A cornerstone of Marvel Comics with over 900 issues of The Amazing Spider-Man.

TV & Animated Legacy

📺 1967 Classic

The first animated series, famous for the memetic theme song. Defined Spider-Man for a generation.

🕸️ 1994 Animated Series

Beloved "Spider-Man: TAS" — deep storytelling, multi-episode arcs, and the iconic "Secret Wars" adaptation.

⚡ Spectacular Spider-Man (2008)

Widely considered the definitive adaptation. Brilliant writing, character development, and fluid animation.

🌌 Ultimate Spider-Man & 2017 Series

Modern takes with team-ups and fresh art styles, introducing new generation to the wall-crawler.

✨ Spider-Verse (Animated Film)

Groundbreaking "Into the Spider-Verse" & "Across the Spider-Verse" — multiverse masterpiece.

Web of Exploration

Live-Action Movies

Tobey Maguire, Andrew Garfield, Tom Holland — three eras of Spider-Man. Iconic moments from Raimi trilogy to MCU's No Way Home.

Rogues Gallery

Venom, Green Goblin, Doctor Octopus, Mysterio, Kraven the Hunter — some of comics' greatest villains.

Allies & Loved Ones

Mary Jane Watson, Gwen Stacy, Miles Morales, Spider-Gwen, Black Cat, and Aunt May — the beating heart.

Global Adaptations

Japanese Spider-Man (1978) with Leopardon mecha! Also Indian Spider-Man (Pavitr Prabhakar) from Spider-Verse.

Video Games

Marvel's Spider-Man (Insomniac) series, Spider-Man 2 (PS5), Ultimate Spider-Man, and the classic PS1 titles.
