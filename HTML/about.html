ABOUT SPIDER-MAN

Who is Spider-Man?

Spider-Man is one of the most iconic superheroes in the world, known for his agility, intelligence, and strong moral values. Behind the mask is Peter Parker, a regular teenager balancing life and responsibility.

Origin Story

Bitten by a radioactive spider, Peter gained superhuman powers. After losing Uncle Ben, he learned the harsh truth of life — responsibility defines a hero.

Powers & Abilities

  • Superhuman Strength
  • Spider-Sense
  • Wall Crawling
  • Web Shooters

Comic History

Created in 1962, Spider-Man became a global icon, shaping generations of fans and redefining superhero storytelling.
